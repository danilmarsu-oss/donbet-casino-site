Recognising Problem Gambling

Problem gambling can affect anyone, regardless of age, background, or experience. We encourage all of our players to regularly assess their gambling behaviour and to look out for the following warning signs:

  • Spending more money on gambling than you can afford to lose
  • Chasing losses in an attempt to win back money
  • Gambling to escape stress, anxiety, or other emotional difficulties
  • Neglecting personal, professional, or family responsibilities due to gambling
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions in order to fund gambling activity
  • Feeling irritable or restless when attempting to cut back on gambling
  • Lying to friends or family members about the extent of your gambling
  • Feeling a loss of control over your gambling habits

If you recognise any of these signs in yourself or someone you know, we strongly encourage you to make use of the responsible gambling tools available on our platform or to reach out to one of the professional support organisations listed below.
